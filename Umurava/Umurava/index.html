Our Story

Mission

Vision

  • Half employers say they cannot find candidates with advanced digital skills. Roles requiring digital skills often pay more than those that do not. As we are building Africa’s Largest Quality talents to service local and global employers, we experience high demand and requirements for our talents to have high-level of digital skills and ability to use digital tools to perform their responsibilities. We have also recorded a high number of talents who failed to meet the employers’ expectation, especially international employers because of the lack of 21st Century skills.

    That is why we have designed a job readiness program that equips young people with 21st-century skills that accelerate their career growth and increase their competencies for navigating the challenges and opportunities available in the current digital age and knowledge economy
  • Empowering young people to thrive in the Knowledge Economy and Future of work.
  • To become the leading job readiness program that equips young individuals with the essential 21st-century skills needed to excel in diverse career paths, fostering their confidence and competence to navigate the challenges and opportunities of the digital age and knowledge economy.
